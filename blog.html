Celebrating SGP’s 30 th Anniversary at UNFCCC COP27 and CBD OP15

At COP27 in Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt and COP15 in Montreal, Canada, the GEF SGP commemorated 30 years of support to local action together with the GEF, UNDP, Civil Society, Indigenous Peoples and other key partners. The events highlighted the programme's delivery of more than USD 720 million in funding to over 27,000 community-based projects across 136 countries.

UN Water Conference 2023- local action and water management

On 23 March, in support of the UN Water Conference, GEF SGP hosted a side event entitled: Local Action in Water Management in Small Island Developing States with a focus on water management in Caribbean SIDS. The event highlighted the need for integrated land and seascape approaches, innovation in water management, citizen science, increased focus on behavioral change strategies, community ownership and the importance of multi-stakeholder partnerships.
